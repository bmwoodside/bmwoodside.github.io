Imperium Community App
(Star Citizen Community App)

I have an amazing opportunity to be able to help support the development and growth of our Star Citizen organization - Imperium - with this great webapp. While I'm not one of the original developer members, I've joined the technology team so that I can help make our community app even better. Currently my role is focused around modernizing the codebase by migrating everything from legacy versions of Laravel up to the most recent LTS release version. Additional projects spawn off of this to better support our organization; as we continue to grow and have a presence in the community we have ongoing needs for automation and discord integration / bot creation, data science opportunities, and more, and this modernization effort is a requirement to be able to support these growing needs.

Technologies used:

Laravel, PHP, jQuery, MySQL/Laravel ORM, Docker
